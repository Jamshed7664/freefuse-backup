Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


  • Click the camera icon in the upper right corner. If you do not have an account, you will be prompted to sign up. If you do have an account, you will be lead to the uploading mechanic screen where you can create content.

  • You can upload as many videos as needed for your project on the initial video uploading screen.

  • Choose “Create Interactive Content” in order to make content. You will be lead to a screen featuring all of your videos where you can choose which video you would like to start your video with. After choosing that video, a node will appear. You can then use that node to create your content through pathways.

  • Using the drop down menu within the node screen, you can choose any videos that you wish to connect to each node.

  • To add more nodes, click “add choice” and up to 4 connections can be added. This can be done as many times as necessary in order to create the content that you want.

  • If you would like to preview the video before publishing, click “preview” to play the piece of content from the beginning.

  • When you are ready to publish, you can click “publish” to bring a menu with a box for the description and tags to categorize the content.

  • You can upload a thumbnail using the “Upload Thumbnail” button for better presentation of your videos.

  • When the video reaches the yellow marker, 1-4 buttons will appear that you can interact with in order to influence the path of the content. Click on which path that you would like to take in order to make it to the next part of the content personalized to your choice. This will be updated with a more seamless AI as we continue development.

  • Yes, due to the unique uploading mechanic you can edit your videos after you’ve published them. Go to “My videos” from the “My Stuff” drop down menu to go to your videos. A selection of your drafts and published videos will be located there. For the video that you wish to edit, click “edit video” to open up the uploading mechanic with the structure you previously created in place. You can then add or delete parts of that video as you would like.

  • Yes, using the “Share” button on the bottom left of the uploading mechanic, you can invite other users to upload videos to the uploading mechanic and edit the content.

  • The decision of ownership and the intellectual property will be left to the original creator who invited the others to collaborate. FreeFuse will not mediate any disputes between creators.

  • Currently, you can sign up using your Google and Facebook accounts. More accounts will be added later.

  • Through some APIs in development, you can share this content on Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Reddit, and other media sites.

  • You can share content with other users on the platform as well. They will receive a notification when you have shared the content with them and they can interact with it from there.

  • Audience Participation live streaming will be available once we are past the beta phase of development.

  • The mobile application will be available during the beta phase of development.

  • You can delete videos by going to “My videos” and deleting the video that you wish to delete.

  • You can choose what parts of your profile that you want others to see by going to your settings. Go to “Privacy”. At this screen, you can choose what others see publicly.
